Publisher: What is it?

BACKGROUND INFORMATION FOR LEARNERS

 

Microsoft Publisher is one of the desktop publishing software available that focuses on page layout and design[1].

The difference between Word Processing and Desktop Publication is that Word Processing programs focus on paperwork usually found in offices and schools. In contrast, Desktop Publication software focuses on making marketing materials and advertisement paraphernalia to persuade people and inform them. That is why documents that focus on designs should be produced on desktop publishing software instead of word processing programs.

There are known popular programs in desktop publishing such as Microsoft Office Publisher, Corel Draw, and Adobe InDesign. Among these, MS Publisher is the most commonly used by publishers because of its basic and user-friendly features.
